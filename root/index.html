Welcome to the Hotel Fantastic!

Directed by Megan Livingston and Ben Abraham

March 26th through April 17th

Immerse yourself in the new, virtual wonderland brought to you by The Scattered Players. Welcome to the Hotel Fantastic, a haven of haunts existing somewhere between tomorrow and yesterday. The Hotel ignites the fire of creativity in artists to whom time is determined to misremember. Meet the staff, interact with guests and witness breathtaking performances all from the comfort of your own space. Welcome to the Hotel Fantastic! An immersive, musical sojourn through loss, legacy and desire.
